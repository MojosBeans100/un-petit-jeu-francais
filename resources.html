So you want to learn French?

Here are some facts about the French language:

  • The French language is the 5th most spoken language in the world
  • More than 75 million people speak French as their first language
  • Over 200 million people speak French as a second or third language
  • French is the official language in 13 countries

So you won't be alone on your language journey..

Here are some tips we can give you:

  • Start by learning the French alphabet - it is not dissimilar to the English alphabet but it is fundamental to learn how to pronounce each letter
  • Try to learn at least 10 new words a day - this will only take up 5 minutes at the start of your day and end of your day - that's nothing!
  • Learn a mixture of verbs, nouns and adjectives
  • Speak immediately - this sounds daunting, but you will have to start speaking at some point. Give yourself a headstart by talking as soon as you can string together a sentence, even if it's to yourself in the shower!
